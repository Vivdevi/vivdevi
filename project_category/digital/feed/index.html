BMW Electrified

THE CHALLENGE

With the addition of more electric vehicles being added to the BMW range, the brand wanted offer prospects an easy way to compare their fuel savings by driving electric. With two cost comparison websites already in existence, BMW tasked Juice to combine them together and deliver a single, rich experience for users.

Both websites had different user journeys and calculation inputs, the challenge for the product team was to identify the key features and develop a seamless user journey, one that made it easy for users to locate their model of interest and calculate their fuel saving results.

THE SOLUTION

The product team began by ideating the information hierarchy, taking key data points from both of the previous websites. This began to form the skeleton of a user flow and key product features which were visualised in low-fidelity wireframes. The calculators were a main focus of the product, allowing users to enter their journey details and calculate their all-electric or plug-in-hybrid savings.

Working closely with BMW, the product team were able to identify the data requirements for the calculations, which included complex performance data for each electrified model in the BMW range.

WE DELIVERED

From product inception to final delivery, the team developed a responsive microsite for BMW. Complete with rich micro interactions and a user-centric design, BMW Electrified helps to address a key barrier to purchase for many would-be electrified vehicle owners, around charging costs and range anxiety.

BMW iX Design & Technical Films

The new all-electric BMW iX is available to order now. And what a stunning piece of the future it is.

A few months ago we were privileged enough to meet the iX up close. BMW UK asked us to chat with Product Manager, Sukh Bhamra, and film a series of talking head pieces about the design and technical features of the car.

New Car Locator

PROJECT

BMW New Car Locator

CATEGORY

Application Development
Sales Enablement
Customer Engagement

CLIENT

BMW

THE CHALLENGE

BMW Group’s best kept secret was the range of product they had available for immediate delivery to customers in the UK. You could build a car online or physically visit your local dealer, but there was no way to view available new car stock digitally in the UK. Our challenge was to surface these cars to customers who didn’t wish to wait for built-to-order stock by location and distance.

THE SOLUTION

By plugging into a number of API’s, we were able to design a mobile-up dynamic web application tool that enabled a customer to flexible choose between selecting a series or body style, or by entering their available monthly budget to discover which vehicles were available to them. A customer could compare selected vehicles, explore the fitted options and even reserve their desired vehicle directly.

THE RESULT

Since launch in July 2020, BMW & MINI New Car Locator has had over:

  • 1,208,727 pages viewed
  • 163,500 unique user visits
  • 248,703 sessions

BMW

Test Your Drive

THE CHALLENGE

Explaining the PHEV proposition is complex and lies in how each of us drive our vehicles, our unique journey distances, charging availability, frequency and commute times. All of these elements affect potential cost savings when compared against traditional petrol and diesel cars. Coupled with a need to increase sales of Petrol Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) and promote awareness of the full PHEV range, BMW needed a digital platform to communicate this tricky message.

THE SOLUTION

Working with BMW AG enabled us to define the data required to identify short, standard and occasional driving styles, which allows users to choose the most relevant to them. We then combined fuel savings with VED savings to provide a clear illustration of savings for the consumer.

TestYourDrive delivers the key information succinctly and clearly to provide customers with all the info they need from one site. Mobile first and ‘future-proofed’ it is ready to expand with the BMW PHEV range over the coming years…

ASDA

ASDA Think Programme

PROJECT

Asda Think Programme

CATEGORY

Corporate Film & Video
Animation
Application Development
Video Learning
Employee Engagement
Gamification

CLIENT

ASDA

THE CHALLENGE

ASDA Grocery wanted to transform their existing training for Delivery Drivers from a predominately classroom-based, practical and lengthy PDF guide over a two-week period to a bite-size, engaging online experience. The experience needed to deliver consistency across the board whilst ensuring that key checks and measures were followed alongside delivering the ASDA customer promise of Easy, Fast and Friendly.

THE SOLUTION

To work with existing content and a dedicated group of subject matter experts within ASDA Grocery, we established a visual concept around the ASDA ‘Think’ programme. Consisting of 8 chunks of micro-learning, accessed across all devices, using a range of media to deliver key messaging and training. Think Online was the hero piece aimed at employees within ASDA grocery, positioning the importance of Online Grocery alongside all aspects of a Delivery Driver’s training – from a day in the life, through to topics such as measurement, customer service and how to build resilience in roles.

A blended approach of videos, animation, interactive exercises and knowledge checks all formed part of the experience for delivery drivers setting them up for success in either their new role or to serve as a refresher for existing drivers.

WE DELIVERED

Less is more, simple language backed up with visually impactful design gives a fresh approach to training whilst catering for differences in language and learning capabilities.Branching out into digital was a big step for training within the business so our solution needed to be sympathetic to this and keep the audience always at the heart of the piece.

IHG Podium Predictor

BEAT THE PUNDIT FOR YOUR CHANCE TO WIN!

PROJECT

IHG Rewards Podium Predictor

CATEGORY

Application Development
Customer Engagement
Gamification

CLIENT

IHG

THE CHALLENGE

How do you bring two brands and their fans together, plus increase membership of the world’s largest hotel loyalty programme all within a motorsport’s season?

IHG wanted to increase membership of it’s IHG Rewards Club programme and raise awareness of their involvement with global motorsport, via it’s partnerships with IMSA WeatherTech Sportswear Championship and the British Touring Car Championship. With two fan bases, multiple decision-makers, legal essentials around sweepstakes and age restrictions plus a launch timeframe rushing towards us; we had our work cut-out.

THE SOLUTION

Build an online result predictor for both IHG Rewards Club members and non-members, to beat a pundit’s predictions on all individual races in the BTCC or IMSA 2017 series. And to incentivise take-up, winners would be chosen from a prize draw and receive prizes of IHG Rewards Club points. All they would need to do was enter their predictions and register with IHG’s Rewards Club.

WE DELIVERED

A mobile first web application for both BTCC and IMSA, addressing the fan bases of both of IHG’s global partners in motorsport.

BMW LaunchPad

HOW CAN WE SUPPORT THE RETAILER NETWORK?

PROJECT

BMW LaunchPad

CATEGORY

Corporate Film & Video
Application Development
Sales Enablement
Video Learning
Employee Engagement
Internal Communications

CLIENT

BMW

THE CHALLENGE

Now and again we are quite opinionated. Nothing, maybe aside from running out of the finest wood roasted coffee beans, really irritates us more than ‘classic’ creative agency behaviour – taking clients money to make stuff, but then having no clue how that stuff is delivered to the end user and caring even less. Juice, powered by coffee, has always been about both – making and delivering. Creating stuff for BMW is thrilling, but we (and they) felt quickly, that the quality of content being produced needed a proper platform to deliver it quickly and effectively into the hands of the users.

THE SOLUTION

Lovely as it is to sit in the office and pontificate about our brilliance, we knew user engagement was key, so we went on a road trip speaking to all levels of employee in the BMW retailer network so as to ensure we understood their requirements fully. We combined these insights with those of BMW HQ to shape an approach to deliver product information quickly and effectively. Our aim was simple – deliver a mobile-up comms platform, accessible from anywhere, updatable instantly, with full reporting and analytics so we could learn what content worked. The Juice Geeks loved us – especially when we mumbled ‘4 months to pilot’.

WE DELIVERED

BMW LaunchPad, a web-app providing rich media, film, web and PDF content to support the retailer network. LaunchPad has become the one-stop place that the network engages with – every day (yes, they do log in on Christmas day). Updated constantly and quickly the platform houses key product information and customer facing and shareable content, as well as being adopted by pretty much all other BMW UK departments. With the inclusion of advanced reporting for the first time, BMW have a clear insight into which content is being viewed, when and by whom, thus guiding our creation process. Bang 4 Buck ™.

Alphabet Virtual Tour

A TOTALLY PERSONALISED DIGITAL COMPANION

PROJECT

Alphabet Virtual Tour

CATEGORY

Application Development
Customer Engagement
Employee Engagement
Internal Communications

CLIENT

Alphabet

THE CHALLENGE

Alphabet (the BMW owned one, not the parent company of Google just for clarity – brand squatters that they are) recently moved into swanky new offices in Farnborough, which are both very white, and, pretty much impossible to navigate yourself around (we know this as it’s where BMW lived for a bit and we could never find anyone). Anyway, they wanted to create a cool, personalised and on-brand experience for visitors to their offices. Obviously they called us. We know ‘cool’ and ‘offices’ having one ourselves.

Communicating the people and experience behind the job titles.

THE SOLUTION

Who wants a printed map of people and their locations which goes out of date in ten minutes (assuming everyone is at tea break for at least nine of those ten minutes). Dull, curly-edged and useless. We wanted to not only de-mystify the office, but bring the people of Alphabet out from behind their job titles. We wanted it to be used pre, during and post visit, and allow the user to take a virtual tour of the whole big, white, shiny, place.

WE DELIVERED

A totally personalised digital companion usable on iPad or smart phone from which a link to a tour can be emailed to customer prior to arrival – taking them through their virtual visit It provides key information on the Alphabet teams and individuals and departments – with the user even choosing who to virtually ‘meet’. Completely updatable for Alphabet themselves to keep key contact/department structures up to date.
