My BMW App Animation

THE CHALLENGE

BMW wanted to communicate the launch of their all new MyBMW app.  A step forward from the existing ConnectedDrive app and one that would eventually replace its predecessor.  The launch was significant as it sets the foundations of their connected ownership experience, yet the messaging had to be subtle as many of the new features would be incremental and arrive in the future.

Our challenge – to create a benefit led film that drives home the features and benefits of the app, encouraging existing BMW owners to download MyBMW and be better connected to their car than ever before.

THE SOLUTION

To create an animated showcase, in keeping with the animated world we created for the New Car Locator animation (https://stock.bmw.co.uk), yet distinctly separate, to provide a concise overview of features and benefits of the MyBMW App. Using carefully crafted messaging and animation, the film will effectively communicate key customer benefits and the added value the App will bring to their driving experience. Additionally, the film will talk to retailers in terms of Service Booking Online and the opportunity for a closer connection to their customers over the course of their vehicle ownership. 

WE DELIVERED

From concept to storyboarding we delivered a perfectly blended combination of crafted designs, text and engaging 2D and 3D animation to be viewed throughout BMW showrooms, on their social channels and within their internal comms platforms.

Content that works seamlessly to support the customer during the ownership of their vehicle and which promotes direct and on-going contact with the retailer.

BMW New Car Locator Animation

THE CHALLENGE

To promote the launch of BMW’s New Car Locator website – a big step for the brand – and one that would enable customers to search for available stock cars in the UK. With lockdown in full swing at the time, our challenge was to create an engaging animated piece that depicts the key features and mechanics of the site. The final version needed to be edited into shorter versions which would be used to promote the new website across multiple social media platforms.

An animated showcase of the different stages of the site in an abstract and contemporary manner yet illustrating the simplicity of the user experience journey on the website. From the number of vehicles available to the filtering of location, price and even colour, we wanted to highlight that a new BMW was only a few clicks away.

WE DELIVERED

An extended animation version for the BMW.co.uk website and provided socially optimised versions for Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn.

Odeon Safety & Pre-Show Animation

THE CHALLENGE

Odeon are the largest group of cinemas in Europe operating across 12 countries. However like many entertainment businesses they had to completely cease their operations at speed as COVID-19 hit. Months of work followed behind the scenes to implement shifting Government guidelines preparing hygiene, sanitation and social distancing measures throughout the whole customer journey. It’s not often we get a one-line brief from a client – but this is what it said ‘Winning the trust of the cinema-going public is at stake’. Oh and we need to move crazy fast (and with total confidentially as this is an extremely sensitive subject). Lights, camera….animation!

THE SOLUTION

Knowing that the public are being bombarded by similar safety messages, we felt the pressure to make something stand out and dare we say it ‘filmic’– there’s only so many films of people looking miserable in masks we could bear and they are not for us. Odeon are understandably passionate about film (as are we it’s fair to say), so we reckoned this needed to pay homage to some classic movie moments using a negative space illustration style (can you spot all 17 nods?) oh, and work across territories, languages and different brand colours across the business. A result only possible with continuous and close cooperation across the Juice team and a dedicated, engaged client.

WE DELIVERED

A 60 second animation for all social platforms as well as website and CRM. They loved it so much we were asked to create a second version for in-cinema use – you’ll now see it played before every film the group shows and it’s officially the first Juice Cinema ad! With both versions being translated into 10 languages for European use it’s next stop The Oscars. Maybe.

PROJECT

Odeon Safety Animation

CATEGORY

Animation
Customer Engagement
Internal Communications

CLIENT

Odeon Cinema Group

Client Feedback

“You’ve taken the subject of sanitisers, spacing and sickness and elevated it to a visually dramatic and heartfelt piece of art. I mean, who does that?!

Once again, you have the (uncanny) knack of putting onto screen what I can see in my head. And – thankfully – making it better than I could’ve imagined. Thank you.”

David Anderson. Group Chief Commercial Officer,
Odeon Cinemas Group.

IHG Responsible Procurement

THE CHALLENGE

Underpinning IHG’s purpose, strategy, reputation and ability to deliver long-term shareholder returns is their commitment to responsible business. A key component of this commitment is how IHG manages their complex supply chain, ethical standards and green criteria – referred to as Responsible Procurement.

Our challenge was to demystify Responsible Procurement and position this globally to the business, suppliers and hotels to kick-start an ongoing engagement and learning comms programme that would affect change and support IHG’s 2018-2020 responsible business targets.

THE SOLUTION

To enable a global audience to start to understand what Responsible Procurement looks like and how this applies to them in their day-to-day roles, we needed to capture what is a very complex subject and translate this into a simplified visual and written language. This would form the foundation for future ongoing sustainment programmes and initiatives.

WE DELIVERED

An articulation of what Responsible Procurement is, at a high-level, was needed as a starting point. Once defined, this would be applied across all communications to deliver a consistent, overarching message from which more detailed subject matter would be developed.

Asda Think Programme

CATEGORY

Corporate Film & Video
Animation
Application Development
Video Learning
Employee Engagement

CLIENT

ASDA

THE CHALLENGE

ASDA Grocery wanted to transform their existing training for Delivery Drivers from a predominately classroom-based, practical and lengthy PDF guide over a two-week period to a bite-size, engaging online experience. The experience needed to deliver consistency across the board whilst ensuring that key checks and measures were followed alongside delivering the ASDA customer promise of Easy, Fast and Friendly.

THE SOLUTION

To work with existing content and a dedicated group of subject matter experts within ASDA Grocery, we established a visual concept around the ASDA ‘Think’ programme. Consisting of 8 chunks of micro-learning, accessed across all devices, using a range of media to deliver key messaging and training. Think Online was the hero piece aimed at employees within ASDA grocery, positioning the importance of Online Grocery alongside all aspects of a Delivery Driver’s training – from a day in the life, through to topics such as measurement, customer service and how to build resilience in roles.

A blended approach of videos, animation, interactive exercises and knowledge checks all formed part of the experience for delivery drivers setting them up for success in either their new role or to serve as a refresher for existing drivers.

WE DELIVERED

Less is more, simple language backed up with visually impactful design gives a fresh approach to training whilst catering for differences in language and learning capabilities.Branching out into digital was a big step for training within the business so our solution needed to be sympathetic to this and keep the audience always at the heart of the piece.

BMW MOT Protect

THE CHALLENGE

No one wants the bother of a pesky MOT when you buy a shiny if slightly used car from a renowned retailer. Buy a motor from a backstreet ‘under the arches’ kind of guy you would know it’s dodgy and likely to have been made from several different vehicles, but the experience in a shiny BMW showroom should be rather different. MOT Protect sounds properly boring and doesn’t really explain anything. Explain it and make it interesting was the brief.

THE SOLUTION

We needed something that had longevity – BMW has a habit of updating their cars every few months so filming would have been a stupid idea – and there was no existing material. Animation was the answer with easily modified on-screen text as and when things changed. Best to watch it really as I don’t think you want to read about what it is here do you?

WE DELIVERED

We created both BMW and MINI versions using 3D animation. Concept, storyboarding, scripting, motion graphics all done in house by our awesome team! Designed to be sent to customers as well as played in showrooms – happily they loved it so much it soon appeared on bmw.co.uk and social channels.

BMW i3 – Handover

THE CHALLENGE

Simply put. Change the buying behaviour of the car buying public. Mmmmmm. Thanks for that one. So, whilst there was significant investment in above the line and lots of PR noise around the launch of the i3, it didn’t, how we can we put this, really explain the considerable and quantifiable benefits of buying one. So whilst the ‘green brigade’ marched straight into their nearest BMW retailer and ordered one on the spot, the challenge laid at our door was to help the retailers persuade lots of other people to join the orderly queue.

THE SOLUTION

Let’s be honest, one of the challenges of following behind the car launch is cash. It’s basically all been gobbled up and spent on posters and pesky, questionably effective stuff, like TV ads and millions of Facebook ads that really irritate the viewer. So, we knew we had to work with what footage we already had, and that meant a chance to layer in some super-cool animation over the top and make it look bespoke, instead of reheated.

WE DELIVERED

Award-winning. That’s a nice thing to write. But arguably demonstrably effective is even better. And to top that all we delivered something that gets the messages, complex as they are, clearly and quickly across and the retailers loved (partly because it helps them understand it too…). Watch the film and realise that the i3 is the future. Buy one and ensure you have one, too. And more friends. And less time in petrol stations (sorry BP, we know you’re a client too).

PROJECT

BMW i3 Handover

Animation
Customer Engagement

CLIENT

BMW

Sophie Chiappe, BMW.

IHG Pace

THE CHALLENGE

The first challenge was arguably the hardest – answer the phone to Procurement without feeling nervous. Once we had got over that and realised that actually they were universally lovely people who just wanted more friends the whole thing became easier. The next was easier – helping the thousands of colleagues who use the new on-line procurement platform understand it, embrace it and ideally almost love it.

THE SOLUTION

There are few businesses where the Procurement department are first on the Christmas party list – but the IHG team really wanted to reset their relationship with those who traditionally had probably tried to work around it, rather than with it. So we needed to appeal to heads and hearts – not only helping with the technicalities of the new system, but also changing perceptions of the whole department to one of helpful expertise which could reduce the stress of finding and working with key suppliers.

WE DELIVERED

Pretty much created a ‘brand’ for the team using a vividly coloured animation with voice over and a learning programme delivered on the IHG LMS. Surprising, helpful and fun – we used the IHG brand palette (there’s a lot of mango) throughout – the client loved it so much that it is being used on further collateral to promote the system. As with all good projects, this is an organic one so we are currently working on a 2017 update.
