Dunlop Moto – Brand Identity

THE CHALLENGE

Dunlop is an icon of the automotive world – indeed, John Boyd Dunlop invented the world’s first pneumatic tyre all the way back in 1888. They’ve lasted so long because they put riders at the heart of everything they do – so when they asked us to refresh their motorcycle tyre brand in 2020, we knew exactly what to do.

WE DELIVERED

We established a brand look and feel that really puts the viewer in the boots of the rider. Bold, active headlines talk about the rider’s experience, not the product features. And all summed up by the new brand motto, ‘Just Ride’ – a promise to the rider that Dunlop have their back, so they can just concentrate on the thrill of the ride.

We’ve rolled out the ‘Just Ride’ brand ethos across the Dunlop moto website, product guides, social media and press advertising. Making sure Dunlop are all set to remain the rider’s choice for motocycle tyres, long into the future.

Reaction Engines – Brand Identity

THE CHALLENGE

We’ve been brand partners with Reaction Engines for six years – helping them tell the story of an engineering firm (revolutionising the space industry) that tripled in size to become an organisation with global reach. But they needed a new, inspirational brand strategy to take them beyond the aerospace sector and help them achieve ambitious commercial targets.

WE DELIVERED

The answer? Elevate the Reaction Engines brand beyond the purely technological space and focus instead on the transformative potential of their work. Giving the whole organisation greater confidence and an improved reputation with their high-profile target audience.

All summed up in a simple, inspirational and powerful brand promise: making beyond possible.

Grenadier – Brand Identity

THE CHALLENGE

INEOS Automotive wanted to fill a gap in the market for a workhorse 4×4. They needed a partner to help get the brand – and the business – off the ground.

WE DELIVERED

We helped build the Grenadier brand from the ground up. Working closely with the business from the very beginning, we were determined to buck industry conventions. So we steered away from the crowded lifestyle market and first focused instead on a ‘utilitarian’ audience – farmers, foresters, estate managers.

We then established a brand positioning that planted the Grenadier firmly in this territory. This led us to a bold look and feel, and a no-nonsense tone of voice – all summed up by the ‘built on purpose’ mantra that hammers home the vehicle’s utilitarian character.

Our work for the INEOS Grenadier didn’t stop at brand launch. We made sure the world took notice and future owners were kept engaged by delivering ongoing engaging brand content.

We created a behind-the-scenes ‘Building the Grenadier’ short film series that tells the story of the vehicle, component by component. Each highly engaging, presenter-fronted episode reveals a key part – from frame and axles to engine, wheels and beyond – to give viewers the complete picture on what they can expect from this rugged 4×4. Visiting engineering partners, testing, factories, and talking to those in charge, and keeping people up to date on all the latest developments as the Grenadier takes shape.
